Gulab jamun

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • All-purpose flour

Recipe

  1. Homemade gulab jamun is usually made up of khoya, a pinch of all-purpose flour
  2. baking powder and clarified butter (ghee); milk kneaded to form a dough,
  3. moulded into balls, deep fried and dropped into simmering sugar syrup.

Khoa Barfi

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • Khoa
  • Dry fruits

Recipe

  1. To prepare barfi, khoa (condensed milk) and granulated sugar are mixed and cooked together.
  2. When the mixture thickens to a fudge-like consistency, it is spread onto a greased plate.
  3. After cooling, it is cut into squares, diamonds, or circles

Butter chicken

Ingredient

  • Butter
  • chicken
  • Spices

Recipe

  1. Chicken is marinated for several hours in a mixture of lemon juice, dahi (yogurt),
  2. Kashmiri red chili, salt, garam masala, ginger paste, and garlic paste.
  3. The marinated chicken is cooked in a tandoor (traditional clay oven),
  4. but may be grilled, oven-roasted, or pan-fried.
